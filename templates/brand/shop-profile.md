# Yohaku — ショッププロフィール文(Etsy開設時にそのまま使用)

声紋「静かな職人」準拠。英語。Etsy開設フォームの各欄に貼る。
ショップ名が YohakuStudio 以外に決まった場合は、文中の "Yohaku" は屋号そのものなので変更不要。

## Shop title / タグライン(55文字以内、ショップ名の下に出る一文)
```
Calm digital goods rooted in Japanese design
```
(代替: `Printables & SVGs shaped by Japanese aesthetics`)

---

## Announcement(ショップ告知。トップに表示)
```
Welcome to Yohaku.

"Yohaku" means the empty space in a design — the quiet room that lets
everything else breathe. Every file here is made in that spirit: calm,
useful, and ready to print or use today.

New designs are added each week. Thank you for visiting.
```

---

## About / Story(ショップの物語。"About this shop" セクション)
```
I'm a maker in Japan, drawn to the kind of design that says less so it can
mean more — the negative space (yohaku) at the heart of Japanese aesthetics.

This shop is where that idea becomes things you can actually use: minimalist
planners, traditional Japanese patterns, and seasonal art, prepared with care
and a steady eye for quality.

Some designs draw on patterns like asanoha (the hemp leaf) that have marked
Japanese craft for centuries. Others are built for calm, focused everyday use.
All of them are made to be quiet, clear, and genuinely useful.

✦ About my process: these designs are created with the assistance of AI tools,
with human curation, refinement, and quality review at every step.
```

## ポリシー文の方向(開設時にClaudeが各欄を埋める)
- Digital downloads: instant delivery, no physical item shipped
- Returns: デジタル商品のため返金不可。ただし問題があればメッセージで必ず対応する旨を温かく書く
- Licensing: 個人利用+小規模物販可、デジタルファイルの再販・再配布は不可(SVGバンドルは商用可の範囲を商品ごとに明記)
